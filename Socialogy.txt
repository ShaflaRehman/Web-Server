Sociology examines the structures, relationships, and dynamics within societies, offering a lens through which to understand human interactions and social phenomena. Social movements, as collective efforts to promote or resist change, play a critical role in shaping societal progress and addressing injustices. From the Civil Rights Movement to contemporary environmental activism, these movements reflect societal values and conflicts, driving social transformation. Analyzing the origins, strategies, and impacts of social movements provides insight into the mechanisms of social change and the ongoing struggle for a more equitable and just world.
